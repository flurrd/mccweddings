A Celebration of Life or Funeral Service can help you say goodbye and thank you to your loved one.

My service provides warmth, comfort and support by remembering your loved one, and remembering them well! We all need to say good-bye respectfully whilst honouring that spirit that has passed.

We can do this by paying tributes, acknowledging and focusing on their life, their achievements, beliefs and loves.

A celebration will be accompanied with beautiful readings and poetry, moving music, honouring words from family and loved ones and symbolism that represents your loved ones life story.

Together we can create a heart felt, authentic, uplifting and memorable service that reflects the qualities and attributes of your beloved.
